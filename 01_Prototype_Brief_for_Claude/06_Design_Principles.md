# Design Principles

- Use DetectedX only as a reference environment.
- Do not copy DetectedX directly.
- Use a dark clinical viewer style.
- Keep the mammogram image area dominant.
- Make the AI layer soft, not alarming.
- Use neutral, non-blaming language.
- Do not build a full medical system.
- Do not build a full DICOM viewer.
- Use static images and simulated viewer controls.
- Prioritise the service design narrative over technical complexity.
