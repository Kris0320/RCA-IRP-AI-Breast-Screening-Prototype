# Whiteboard to Wireframe Notes

## Frame 8 — Reason Confirmation Micro-interaction

- This replaces the earlier “Learning Tag” idea.
- It belongs after final decision, not during routine reading.
- It reduces manual burden by turning long reasoning into a suggested tag.
- The confirmed reason is saved into the Non-blame Decision Receipt.
- The actual Learning Value Check happens later, after follow-up outcome.

### Design intention

Frame 8 reframes the tag as `Confirm Suggested Reason` / `Reason for Decision`, rather than a learning task. The interaction sits after consensus / arbitration review and before the auto-generated decision record is saved.

This keeps the routine reading workflow lighter: the reviewer confirms or edits a suggested rationale, while learning value is assessed later through outcome-linked review.

### Visual coding

- Blue label: Reviewer action — confirm or edit reason
- Green label: System support — suggests reason from case context
- Orange label: Output — rationale saved to non-blame decision record
