## What this snapshot contains
This snapshot compares several AI breast screening workflow placements. Visible diagrams include "AI as Independent Reader", "AI as Supporting Reader", a screening and arbitration workflow, and a pre-triage group pathway. The board also separates task flow, tools flow, and information flow, with sticky notes about disagreement moments, arbitration, and recording.

## Why it matters to the IRP project
This snapshot directly supports the project's workflow comparison work. It shows that AI-human disagreement changes depending on whether AI replaces a reader, supports a reader, or appears before human reading as triage. It also connects disagreement to arbitration workload, what information is visible, what gets recorded, and how AI-human conflict becomes a design opportunity.
