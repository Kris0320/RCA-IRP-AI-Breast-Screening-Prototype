# Disagreement Handling Logic

## Core sequence
Detect -> Classify -> Escalate -> Resolve -> Record -> Learn

## Detect
Identify AI-human or human-human disagreement.

## Classify
Classify disagreement type, uncertainty and risk.

## Escalate
Trigger second look, arbitration or senior review.

## Resolve
Create a human-led final decision.

## Record
Capture AI output, human judgement, override reason, timestamp and actor.

## Learn
Feed difficult or unresolved cases into review, calibration or training.
