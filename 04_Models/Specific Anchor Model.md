# Specific Anchor Model

## Working choice
AI as second reader + human-led arbitration.

## Why this model
This model makes the AI-human disagreement moment explicit and gives us a concrete point for service intervention.

## Core logic
Mammogram  
-> Human reader  
-> AI as second reader / evidence layer  
-> Comparison  
-> Disagreement detected  
-> Arbitration / senior review  
-> Final decision  
-> Audit / learning
