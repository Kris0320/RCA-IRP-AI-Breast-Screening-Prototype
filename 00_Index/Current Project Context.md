## Current IRP Project Focus

The IRP is designing a **disagreement handling and learning system for AI-assisted breast screening**.

Current HMW: how might we help breast screening teams detect, resolve and learn from AI-human disagreements in a way that is accountable, transparent and consistent?

Core direction:
- Focus on **AI-human reader disagreement**, not AI accuracy alone.
- Use [[Universal Disagreement Pattern Map]] for comparison.
- Anchor the intervention in [[Specific Anchor Model]]: **AI as second reader + human-led arbitration**.
- Use [[Disagreement Handling Logic]] to structure the service: detect, classify, escalate, resolve, record, learn.
- After [[Tutorial 5 - 2026-06-04]], prototype direction is a **service package**, not just a dashboard.

## Key Decisions

From [[Decision Log]]:

- Use **Universal Disagreement Pattern Map**, not “Universal Workflow”.
- Pattern numbers 1-4 are **placement labels**, not risk rankings.
- Use **span bars** to show workflow segments, not point annotations.
- Anchor intervention in **AI as second reader + human-led arbitration**.
- Show **human judgement** and **AI output** as parallel sources.
- Avoid calling AI a final decision-maker; use “AI output”, “AI risk score”, “AI evidence”, or “AI-supported flag”.
- Prototype as a **service package**, not just a dashboard.
